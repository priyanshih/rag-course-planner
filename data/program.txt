Computer Science Degree Requirements:

- Core courses: CS101, CS201, CS301, CS302
- Advanced electives: any 3 from CS400-level courses
- Math requirement: MATH101, MATH201
- Minimum credits: 120

Students must complete prerequisites before enrolling in advanced courses.

Elective Requirements:
Students must choose electives from advanced CS courses such as CS401–CS450.

Residency Requirement:
At least 60 credits must be completed within the institution.

Repeat Policy:
Courses can be repeated up to two times to improve grades.

Grading Policy:
Minimum grade of C required for prerequisite satisfaction.
