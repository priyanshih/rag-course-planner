Course: Database Management Systems

Prerequisites:
Knowledge of data structures is recommended.

Description:
Covers relational models, SQL, indexing, and normalization.

Course Outcomes:
- Understand fundamental concepts of machine learning
- Apply algorithms to real-world datasets
- Analyze performance of models

This course is part of the NPTEL curriculum and provides structured weekly lectures covering theory and applications. It includes video lectures, quizzes, and assignments designed for practical understanding.
