CS451: Advanced AI Systems

Prerequisite: CS450

Covers large-scale AI systems and optimization.

Course Outcomes:
- Understand core concepts in depth
- Apply theoretical knowledge to practical systems
- Develop problem-solving skills

Detailed Description:
This course provides an in-depth exploration of the subject, including theoretical foundations and practical applications. Students will engage in assignments, projects, and case studies to reinforce learning.

Assessment Methods:
- Assignments: 20%
- Midterm Exam: 30%
- Final Exam: 40%
- Projects/Quizzes: 10%

Learning Resources:
- Standard textbooks and research papers
- Online lecture materials and tutorials

Additional Notes:
Students are expected to actively participate and maintain academic integrity.
